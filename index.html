Tasmanian Bushfires

Tasmania is one of the most naturally diverse places in the world, teeming with native Australian and uniquely Tasmanian wildlife and flora. It hosts some of the cleanest air in the world and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Australia for its beautiful national parks. Being a state dense with national parks and bsuh, Tasmania is a state very susceptible to bushfires. This endangers many native species each year and can wipe out entire tourist destinations, which significantly impacts Tasmania's economy with tourism increasingly making the backbone of Tasmania's ecomomy.
